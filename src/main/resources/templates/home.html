Brazilian E-commerce Public Dataset Information

This dataset contains information about 100,000 orders made at multiple marketplaces over the course of 2 years in Brazil. It also has information about customers, sellers, products, reviews, and the location of both sellers and customers. The data shown on this website will find interesting relationships between the customers and sellers. These relationships will find which products or categories are selling better, how loyal customers are to sellers and how dependable sellers can be, whether good reviews correlate with sales.
